Computatioanl Urban Resilience Lab

Computatioanl Urban Resilience Lab at the University of Texas at San Antonio focuses on the application of AI and urban science towards resilient urban environment and planning

  • Explore More

Aims of Lab

1. Examine Climate-Vulnerable Communities with Urban Data Science

2. Initiate Climate-Resilient Planning in the Context of Global Environmental Change

3. Explore Artificial Intelligence-Enabled Methods for Urban and Regional Planning
